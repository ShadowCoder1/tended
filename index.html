Why Tended exists

The internet has never had a place worthy of these stories

These stories already exist. They're being written in Reddit threads, in CaringBridge comments, in notes apps at 2am by people who needed to put it somewhere. They just don't have a home worthy of them. Tended was built to be that home.

1
Your words stay yours

We review for safety, not style. No editorial polish, no house voice. Every story reads like the person who lived it — because it is.

2
Organized so you can actually find things

By condition, voice type, experience stage. A caregiver's account of late-stage Parkinson's finds exactly that — not just "popular."

3
A community that responds like one

Not "have you tried turmeric?" Real people who have been where you are, saying so.

4
No ads. No sponsors. No selling your story.

These are people's hardest experiences. We will never let that become an inventory.
